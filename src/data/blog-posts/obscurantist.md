While watching the Veritasium video, [There Is Something Faster Than Light](https://youtu.be/NIk_0AW5hFU), there is a quote by John Bell (Physicist, CERN) where he states:
> "I felt that Einstein's intellectual superiority over Bohr, in this instance, was enormous; a vast gulf between the man who saw clearly what was needed and the **obscurantist**." * 

As soon as I heard that word spoken in the video I was alerted.  Could that be a real word?

![78924428-68d3-4ec6-96fe-849e904578cd-image.png](https://d1zd1hdbutccek.cloudfront.net/uploads/a134dd8c-b1fd-4fa2-9f10-9c2e77e24bcb.png) 

All these years, working in IT, and dealing with Obscurantists & I didn't know there was a term for them?!?  🤓

It's even a complete branch of Philosophy!  Well, my years in IT and my experiences with people trying to hide why things work the way they do, seem to back that up.  😆

![20b31f8a-fbbc-4742-ab77-d5f94dbd8291-image.png](https://d1zd1hdbutccek.cloudfront.net/uploads/ccbc0597-ca24-410c-968d-602af09bef6c.png) 

#### Emphasis
> "characterized by **deliberate** vagueness"

Their vagueness wasn't by accident, but purposeful.  

As the antithesis to the Obscurantists out there, I'm glad we cleared that up!  😆😅😂


 *If you [watch the video at this place](https://youtu.be/NIk_0AW5hFU?t=1081), you'll see that many people who've read Bohr's response to the EPR paper find it to be confusing and have purposeful obscurity in it.
